About Namma Kadai

Namma Kadai is a local shop dedicated to bringing essential groceries, personal care items, and daily necessities directly to your doorstep. Our mission is to provide convenience, accessibility, and quality service to our community, especially catering to elderly customers and those unable to visit the shop in person.

Advantages of Our Shop:

  • Door Delivery Service: We deliver directly to your home, saving time and effort.
  • Local & Trusted: Serving the neighborhood with quality products for years.
  • Fresh & Quality Products: We handpick fresh vegetables, fruits, and daily essentials.
  • Reach Nearby Villages: Extending service to nearby rural areas who can’t access big markets.
  • Personalized Service: We help customers choose products, repeat orders, and special requests.
  • Support for Elderly & Busy People: Reducing their need to travel while ensuring timely delivery.
